About the Poet

"In the quiet, words find their pulse."

The Soul Behind Poetry on Pause

A lover of silence, twilight, and the art of almost-saying — the poet behind Poetry on Pause writes not to be understood, but to remember how it feels to feel.

Every poem here is a quiet heartbeat — a reflection of longing, memory, and the beauty hidden in things left unsaid. This space was never meant to be loud — it was meant to breathe.

Words became a home when the world felt too transient. And in these pauses between breaths, you might find a piece of yourself too.
